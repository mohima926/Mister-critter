Find Your Perfect Dog Breed

Golden Retriever

Golden retriever is originated from Scotland, United Kingdom and England. It is very reliable, trustworthy, kind and intelligent.

  • Height is for female varies from 55-57cm
  • Male 58-61cm
  • Weight of male 29.5-34 kg
  • Weight of female 25-32 kg
  • Life span is about 11-12 years
Shih Tzu

It got originated in china. Other names are Chinese Lion Dog and Chrysanthemum Dog. These are playful, outgoing, loyal and gentle.

  • They are litter in size up to 1-8
  • Weight for both female and male varies from 8.8-16 lbs.
  • Height are same for both ranges from 7.9-11 inches.
  • Available in some colors like brindle red, gold
  • Life span of Shi is 10-16 years.
Pug Dog

These are originated in china and when migrated to Europe in sixteenth century it was well popularized. It was developed as passion by Queen Victoria in 19th century. Likewise it was passed to royal family. These types of dogs are strong and aggressive and suitable to grow at houses. These have lazy nature.

  • Height is of 30cm
  • Weight between 14 and 18 pounds (male and female)
  • legs are strong, straight
  • Two distinct shapes for their ears
  • Life span is of 12-15 years
English Mastiff

These dogs breed are originated from England. It is even called as Mastiff or Old English Ma tiff. Its coat is fine and smooth. These are calm, dignified, affectionate, courageous and protective

  • Height varies in between 70-91cm for both female and male
  • Weight of male is greater than female
  • The tail is high-set with a wide base
  • Coat colors include golden fawn, light fawn, apricot, silver, tiger or brindle
  • Life span is about 10-12 years
Border Collie

Border Collie is developed for herding livestock of sheep at boarders. These are acrobatic, energetic, athletic and smart. These are well known as Scottish sheep dog. Death occurs due to cancer, old age and cerebral vascular afflictions

  • Size of male in height 48-56cm and weighs about 13.6-20.4kg
  • Female height is 46-53cm and weight is 12.2-19 kg
  • Athletic, medium-sized herders standing 18 to 22 inches at the shoulder
  • Look like a lighter-weight Australian shepherd
  • Life span is of 13-16 years
English Cocker Spaniel

It was originated in England. Also known as Cocker spaniel. It even called by pet names like Cocker and Cocker spaniel. It is active sporting dog with good-natured. Cockers can be along with children, people and other pets and dogs. This is not best suited for backyard alone

  • Height varies between 38-43cm and in weight about 13-16 kg for male
  • In female weight ranges between 12-15 kg and height of 36-41cm
  • Ears are long and set low
  • Available in some colors like Solid black, liver, red, black and tan, liver and tan, parti-colored, ticked, roan
  • Life span is of 12-15 years
Pomeranian

Pomeranian is named after region Pomeranian in central Europe. It is also called as Deutscher Sptiz. Pet names are pompom, pom and tumbleweed. These became popular by royal owners since the 18th century. It is a breed dog of sptiz type.These type of dog breed is very playful, active, intelligent, extroverted, sociable and friendly.

  • Height is about 20cm
  • Mass of body ranges between 1.9-3.5 kg
  • Long, double coat that comes in many colors
  • Most common colors are red, orange, cream, sable, black, brown, and blue
  • Life span is of 12-16 years
Chow Chow

This breed got originated in china and now known as “Fluffy Lion-dog”. Other names are Tang Quan and Dog of the Tang Quan. These dogs are found as guardians in front of Buddhist temples and places. These are mostly kept as pets. These are loyal, independent, quiet and aloof. Its coat will be thick and coarse

  • Height is common for both male and female that is 17- 20 inches.
  • Weight for the male is 55-70 pounds and for females 45-60 pounds
  • Its litter size is 3-6
  • Most common colors are Red, black, blue, cinnamon, cream
  • Life span is about 9-15 years
Newfoundland

Newfoundland got originated from England. It is a large working dog. These are found in black, brown, grey and land seer. These dogs worked for fishermen. These are well known for tremendous strength, giant size and loyalty. Nicknames are Newf and Newfie.

  • Height and weight for male are 75cm and 60-70 kg
  • Height and weight for female are 68cm and 45-55 kg
  • Coat is thick and straight. Size of litter is 4-12 pups
  • Available in all colours like brown, black and beige
  • Life span is about 8-12 years
Alaskan Malamute

It got originated from the United States Alaska. It is a large breed of domestic dog. Its haul is very freight because of this strength and endurance.

  • Weight of the male is 55 kg, and the female is 38 kg
  • Height of the female is 61cm and male 70cm
  • Size of litter is 4-10 puppies
  • Available in some colors like grey, sable, black or red and white.
  • Life span is up to 16 years
St. Bernard

It got originated from Italy and Switzerland. Other names are St.Bernhardshund, Alpine Mastiff and Bernhardiner. It has a nick name saint

  • Average weight of breed lies between 65-120 kg
  • Average height is 70-90 cm
  • The coat is smoother and rough
  • Available in some colors like Red and white, brindle and white
  • Life span is of 8-10 years
American Eskimo Dog

This dog got originated from Germany. It is a breed of comparion dog and family member of Spitz. It is a toy size dog type.It is reserved, alert, intelligent, protective and friendly.

  • Height is of 23-30 cm
  • Mass of the dog is 2.7-4.5kg
  • Available in white color
  • Coat length is medium
  • Life span is about 13-15 years
