Find Your favorite bird

Oriental Magpie Robin

This bird is about 19 centimeters long. It has black upperparts, head, and throat apart from a white shoulder patch.

Red-capped Robin

A brown bird found in North America. The male has a reddish-brown breast. They are larger than European ones

Blackbird

A European bird, the male of which has black feathers and a bright yellow beak, and the female has brown feathers

Baya Weaver

The Baya Weaver is a bird found across the Indian subcontinent, flocks of these birds are found in grasslands, cultivated areas, and they are best known for their hanging retort-shaped nests woven from leaves.

Drongo

A shiny black bird with a long forked tail with one end divided into two parts, like the shape of the letter ‘Y’ a bird with a forked tail. The forked tongue of snake

Cormorant

A large black bird with a long neck that lives near the sea or other areas of water, and they nest around the shore, on big trees, or on cliffs Cormorants are fish-eaters dining on small eels, fish, and even water snakes.

Parakeet

A small bird of the parrot family, usually with a long tail. Parakeet breed best in groups, but are usually fine breeding in pairs.

Lovebird

A small bird with brightly colored feathers African parrot with 13 to 17 centimeters in length and 40 to 60 grams in weight. They are a social and affectionate small parrot
