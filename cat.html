Find Your Perfect Cat Breed

Abyssinian Cat

Abyssinians are highly intelligent and intensely inquisitive.Lifespan is 9 to 15 years.They have ticked coat.The Abyssinian has a wedge-shaped, yet rounded head with broad ears and almond-shaped eyes in shades of gold or green.

American Bobtail Cat Breed

The American Bobtail is a highly intelligent breed with a clownlike personality. Looking much like a bobtailed wildcat. The American Bobtail has two coat varieties, shorthair and longhair, and does not reach adulthood until 2 or 3 years old.

Bengal Cat

This docile house cat has what some would call a rambunctious personality. Bengal cats are playful and love to chase, climb, investigate and be part of the action. They seek out a lot of attention, are always on the move and can easily get into mischief around your home.

Birman Cat Breed

The Birman doesn’t like being the only animal in the house. She is active and playful but quiet if you are busy. This healthy breed has an outstanding semi-long silky coat that does not mat and a luxurious.Coat are medium to long.Size are upto medium to large

British Shorthair Cat Breed

The British Shorthair is an easygoing feline. She enjoys affection but isn’t needy and dislikes being carried. She’ll follow you from room to room, though, out of curiosity. British Shorthairs aren’t lap cats, but they do enjoy snuggling next to their people on the couch.

Chartreux Cat Breed

The Chartreux has a sweet, smiling expression. This sturdy, powerful cat has a distinctive blue coat with a resilient wooly undercoat. Historically known as fine mousers with strong hunting instincts, the Chartreux enjoys toys that move.C0at are medium.

Devon Rex Cat Breed

The Devon Rex is small to medium, with males weighing 8 to 10 pounds and females weighing 5 to 8 pounds.Their coat are small.They are available in white, black, blue, red, cream, chocolate, lavender, cinnamon and fawn, plus various patterns and shadings

European Burmese Cat Breed

The European Burmese are medium, with males weighing 10 to 14 pounds and females weighing 7 to 10 pounds.Their coat short, fine, glossy.Available in brown, blue, chocolate, lilac, red, cream, brown tortie, blue tortie, chocolate tortie, lilac tortie

Himalayan Cat Breed

The Himalayan Cat are medium to large, with males weighing 9 to 14 pounds and females weighing 7 to 11 pounds.Their coat are long.Available in white to fawn body with color points that include chocolate, seal, lilac, blue, red, cream tortie, blue-cream, chocolate-tortie, lilac-cream, blue

LaPerm Cat

The LaPerm cat are medium, with males weighing from 7 to 10 pounds and females weighing from 5 to 8 pounds.Their coat are short or long, soft, wavy.Available in white, black, blue, red, cream, chocolate, cinnamon, fawn and lavender, plus various patterns and shadings

Persian Cat Breed

The docile Persian is medium to large, with males weighing 9 to 14 pounds and females weighing 7 to 11 pounds.Their coats are long, thick, glossy.Available in White, blue, black, red, cream, chocolate, lilac.Lifespan is about upto 15-20 years.

Turkish Van Cat Breed

The beautiful Turkish Van is medium to large, with males weighing 9 to 20 pounds and females weighing 7 to 12 pound.Their coat are semi-long, fine, cashmere-like.Available in Red, cream, black, blue, red tabby, cream tabby.
